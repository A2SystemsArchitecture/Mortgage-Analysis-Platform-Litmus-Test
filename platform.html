What This Tool Is
HomeTrue is an educational mortgage analysis framework. It produces a structured best-case and worst-case scenario analysis of the estimated monthly costs associated with purchasing a specific residential property in Miami-Dade County, Florida. It is designed to help you understand the full range of your potential monthly financial commitment before making a purchase decision — with no sales pressure and no agenda.
Section 2 of 6
What This Tool Is Not
This tool does not constitute financial advice, legal advice, real estate advice, or mortgage advice. It is not a substitute for consultation with a licensed mortgage broker, real estate attorney, realtor, or insurance professional. The analysis it produces is an educational estimate — not a guarantee, projection, or professional assessment. No output from this tool should be used as the sole basis for any financial decision.
Section 3 of 6
Geographic Scope — Miami-Dade County Only
This tool is calibrated exclusively for properties located in Miami-Dade County, Florida. All financial assumptions, property tax rates, insurance cost ranges, flood zone frameworks, and legislative references are specific to Miami-Dade County. Using this tool for properties outside Miami-Dade County will produce inaccurate results. By proceeding, you confirm the property you are analyzing is in Miami-Dade County.
Section 4 of 6
Accuracy Levels by Tier
This tool operates at three tiers. Tier 1 Pre-Offer: 85–90% accuracy. Tier 2 Active Offer: 92–95% accuracy, requiring a confirmed insurance quote or an acknowledged estimate with quantified variance disclosure. Tier 3 Under Contract: 95–98% accuracy, requiring all confirmed inputs including a locked Loan Estimate. Accuracy decreases when inputs are estimated. Your analysis displays which inputs are confirmed and which are estimated.
Section 5 of 6
Data Currency & Verification Schedule
Mortgage rate data is updated every Friday by 12:00 PM Eastern Time based on the Freddie Mac Primary Mortgage Market Survey published that same Thursday. In the event of a missed Friday update, data will be refreshed by Saturday 12:00 PM Eastern Time. All other financial inputs are verified on the first Friday of each month by 12:00 PM Eastern Time.
