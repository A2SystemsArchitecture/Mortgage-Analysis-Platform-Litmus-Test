Section 1 of 6
What This Tool Is
HomeTrue is an educational mortgage analysis framework. It produces a structured best-case and worst-case scenario analysis of the estimated monthly costs associated with purchasing a specific residential property in your selected county. It is designed to help you understand the full range of your potential monthly financial commitment before making a purchase decision — with no sales pressure and no agenda.
Section 2 of 6
What This Tool Is Not
This tool does not constitute financial advice, legal advice, real estate advice, or mortgage advice. It is not a substitute for consultation with a licensed mortgage broker, real estate attorney, realtor, or insurance professional. The analysis it produces is an educational estimate — not a guarantee, projection, or professional assessment. No output from this tool should be used as the sole basis for any financial decision.
Section 3 of 6
Geographic Scope — Selected County Only
This tool is calibrated for properties located in the county and state you selected. All financial assumptions, property tax rates, insurance cost ranges, flood zone frameworks, and legislative references are specific to your selected county. Using this tool for properties outside your selected county will produce inaccurate results. By proceeding, you confirm the property you are analyzing is located in the county and state you selected above.
Section 4 of 6
Analysis Completeness by Tier
This tool operates at three tiers based on how far along you are in the home buying process. Tier 1 Pre-Offer uses publicly available county tax data and estimated ranges for insurance and other costs — appropriate before you have made an offer. Tier 2 Active Offer incorporates a confirmed or estimated insurance quote, reducing cost variance. Tier 3 Under Contract uses your locked Loan Estimate for the most complete input set. Results improve as you confirm more inputs. Your analysis displays which inputs are confirmed and which are estimated. No tier produces a guarantee or professional assessment.
Section 5 of 6
Data Currency & Verification Schedule
Mortgage rate data is updated every Friday by 12:00 PM Eastern Time based on the Freddie Mac Primary Mortgage Market Survey published that same Thursday. In the event of a missed Friday update, data will be refreshed by Saturday 12:00 PM Eastern Time. All other financial inputs are verified on the first Friday of each month by 12:00 PM Eastern Time.
