Section 1 of 6
What This Tool Is
HomeTrue is an educational mortgage analysis framework. It produces a structured best-case and worst-case scenario analysis of the estimated monthly costs associated with purchasing a specific residential property in Miami-Dade County, Florida. It is designed to help you understand the full range of your potential monthly financial commitment before making a purchase decision — with no sales pressure and no agenda.
Section 2 of 6
What This Tool Is Not
This tool does not constitute financial advice, legal advice, real estate advice, or mortgage advice. It is not a substitute for consultation with a licensed mortgage broker, real estate attorney, realtor, or insurance professional. The analysis it produces is an educational estimate — not a guarantee, projection, or professional assessment. No output from this tool should be used as the sole basis for any financial decision.
Section 3 of 6
Geographic Scope — Miami-Dade County Only
This tool is calibrated exclusively for properties located in Miami-Dade County, Florida. All financial assumptions, property tax rates, insurance cost ranges, flood zone frameworks, and legislative references are specific to Miami-Dade County. Using this tool for properties located outside Miami-Dade County will produce inaccurate results. By proceeding, you confirm that the property you are analyzing is located in Miami-Dade County, Florida.
Section 4 of 6
Accuracy Levels
This tool operates at three accuracy tiers. Tier 1 (Pre-Offer) carries an accuracy target of 85–90% when all required inputs are confirmed. Tier 2 (Active Offer) carries an accuracy target of 92–95% when all required inputs are confirmed, including a real homeowners insurance quote or an acknowledged estimate with a quantified variance disclosure. Tier 3 (Under Contract) carries an accuracy target of 95–98% when all required inputs are confirmed including a locked Loan Estimate from your lender. Accuracy decreases when inputs are estimated. Your analysis will display which inputs are confirmed and which are estimated.
Section 5 of 6
Data Currency & Verification Schedule
Mortgage rate data is updated every Friday by 12:00 PM Eastern Time based on the Freddie Mac Primary Mortgage Market Survey published that same Thursday. In the event of a missed Friday update, data will be refreshed by Saturday 12:00 PM Eastern Time and any analysis generated during that window will display a pending-verification notice. All other financial inputs — property tax rates, flood zone status, insurance market conditions, and PMI ranges — are verified and updated on the first Friday of each month by 12:00 PM Eastern Time. The date and time of the most recent verification is displayed on every output.
